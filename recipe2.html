Three Rivers Bean Dip

  • recipe 1
  • recipe 3
  • Home Page

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 can lima beans, drained
  • 1 can pork and beans
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • tortilla chips
  • sour cream, as topping
  • shredded sharp cheddar cheese, as topping

Instructions:

Brown ground beef and drain. Combine ground beef, chopped onion, chopped green pepper, stewed tomatoes, lima beans, pork & beans, kidney beans, ketchup and brown sugar. Pour into 9x13 baking dish and bake in 375 degree oven for 45 minutes, covered, then cook an additional 10 minutes uncovered. Serve with tortilla chips, shredded cheese and sour cream.

About this Recipe:

The Three Rivers Bean Dip is a perfect dish for game day appetizers, potlucks, or any family and friend gathering. Place it in a crockpot after baking to keep it warm, or serve it directly from the oven./p>

Our Re-S-ipe:

This recipe was first shared by my husband's Aunt Marlene at a family gathering. Everyone loved the flavors and different ways to add toppings. Since then, it has become a staple anytime our family is having an appetizer night or potluck.
